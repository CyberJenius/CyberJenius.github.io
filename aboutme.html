About Me

Christopher (CJ) Joseph Grieco II is a current undergraduate student. He is currently working to recieve a bachelors of science degree in Cybersecurity Operations and Analytics with a degree focus in Application Development. He will graduate in December of 2024 with this degreee, as well as minors in both Information Sciences and Technology and Security Risk Analysis. CJ also will recieve a NSA CAE certificate upon gradutation. This cerificate is provided by the NSA and DHS to recognize Pennsylvania State Universities Cybersecurity program as a center of academic excellence in Cybersecurity.

CJ will also graduate with internship experience working as an IT Helpdesk Technician for the summers of 2023 and 2024. During the school year he is a teaching assistant for Pennsylvania State Universities' College of Information Sciences and Technology, helping to teach students in classes such as decision theory, malware analytics and reverse engineering, and distributed-object computing/web development. In recent months, he has been focusing more on personal programming projects, improving his hacking/pen-testing skills, and working to obtain a CompTIA Security+ Certificate. His love for learning and all things related to technology and computers, as well as his determination to work hard and constantly grow, makes him a perfect fit for the IT, Cybersecurity, and Software Development fields.
